How to ride

Present this image to staff at the bus stop. This bus service is operated free of charge, sponsored by SMS Co., Ltd.

Instead of presenting the image, you can also tell staff to use SMS shuttle bus service (エス・エム・エス シャトルバスを利用します) or RubyKaigi shuttle bus service (RubyKaigi シャトルバスを利用します) to ride.

Busses and bus stops are operated by 三重交通 (Mie Kotsu). Operational status will be updated at Twitter @sms_rk2022 in case of suspension, major delay, or change in service.

Note

  • Buses have no RubyKaigi and SMS Co., Ltd. logo and design.
  • Operating interval may vary depending on actual traffic condition.
  • Don't leave your belongings behind.
  • You may not be able to ride a bus when it's full.
  • Avide by our COVID-19 policy during your ride and protect yourself and others from COVID-19.
  • No foods and drinks allowed inside a bus.
