RubyKaigi 2007

June 10 - 11 9 - 10, 2007, Zendentsu-Rodo-Kaikan Hall in Tokyo, Japan.

("Kaigi" means "conference" in Japanese.)

RubyKaigi2007 is the Ruby conference in Japan (since 2006).

We will have keynotes by Matz and Dave "PragDave" Thomas.

Call for Presentations: RubyKaigi 2007

  • CFP Deadline: January 31, 2007 (JST)

We're opening a call for presentations. We seeks proposals for a wide range of topics about Ruby.

We invite submissions covering all aspects of Ruby.

Our official languages are Japanese and Ruby, But we're welcome to English presentations. (we're now planning some translation work; slide translation, IRC channel, translator ... for the Japanese audience)

We will have talks that last from 15 to 30 minutes, some of these may take more or less time. (we'll determine the final minutes for each talk in the end of selection process).

Proposals should be submitted to mailto:rubykaigi2007@jp.rubyist.net by January 31, 2007 -- midnight JST (Thanks for your cooperation!) is the deadline.

A submission should have:

  • your name and short blurb about you.
  • presentation title.(or working title).
  • short abstract of the talk.
  • necessary minutes for the talk. options here:
    • 15 minutes
    • 30 minutes
    • more or less(how much?)
  • any question.(optional)

The selection process will take place in February, and all submitters will be notified by email by the begin of March.

Important Dates:

  • January 31, 2007
    • Deadline for all submissions
  • Mar 1, 2007
    • Notification of acceptance for submissions
  • June 10-11 9 - 10, 2007
    • Kaigi

Submit to:

  • mailto:rubykaigi2007@jp.rubyist.net

For oversea attendees:

Feel free to contact us -> mailto:rubykaigi2007@jp.rubyist.net
