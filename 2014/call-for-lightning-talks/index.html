Call for Lightning Talks

We RubyKaigi 2014 Team are looking for lightning talks from the attendees/speakers/sponsors of RubyKaigi 2014.
Any topics related to ruby and/or rubyists are welcomed!

  • The lightning talks will be held at 5:30pm on Sep 19, 2nd day at Main Hall.
  • You have exactly 5 minutes to give your talk.
  • You must follow our Anti Harassment Policy
  • You must have your own ticket to RubyKaigi 2014 to give a talk.
  • We won't reimburse your travel expense.
  • Application DEADLINE: 2014-09-04(Thu) 23:59:59(JST)
  • Fields marked with "will be public" are to be public when your proposal is accepted.
  • Remember to KEEP A CARBON COPY OF YOUR FORM INPUTS before pressing the "submit" button.
  • Selection result will be (hopefully) notified until: 2014-09-08(Mon)

We're looking forward to hearing your awesome talk <3
