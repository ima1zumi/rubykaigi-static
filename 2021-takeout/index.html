RubyKaigi goes online again

We RubyKaigi 2021 team have booked the venue to make RubyKaigi 2021 happen in Mie Prefecture this July (with some hope). However, the COVID-19 situation is still unclear and unpredictable so we have decided to give up on holding the large in-person conference. It is really unfortunate that we have to tell you this: RubyKaigi 2021 has been cancelled.

Instead, we're planning an alternate online conference, RubyKaigi Takeout 2021 (as like we've done last year). The schedule is changed from the original RubyKaigi 2021; It will happen in this fall.
